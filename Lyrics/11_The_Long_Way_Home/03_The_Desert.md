# The Desert

**Album:** The Long Way Home  
**Track:** 03  
**Lyrics by:** Nickolay Petrov  
**Written:** 2026

---

A burning earth, and heat that sears the bone...

The air stands still, the sands are scorched and dead.
No shade, no path, no voice in all the land.
The dunes drift on — like waves no river fed —
a shimmering mirage trembles on the sand.

I walk. The sand creaks dryly under boot.
The same fierce heat, the same bleached, blinding light.
The drifting sand conceals my path, my route —
as if I am not here at all, not quite.

But day burned out, and now the heat withdraws.
The sunset spread, a warm and glowing sheet.
A cool breath comes — a gift, a longed-for pause —
and shadows spread their carpet, cool and sweet.

And night descends. The desert, cold and deep.
The sky now glimmers, silver, star on star.
A bottomless vault, hushed, eternal sleep,
the Milky Way spread tent-wide, near and far.

I stand alone beneath the starlit height,
my fear left far behind me, gone, set free.
This land took nothing — gave instead, this night,
the gift to be. To breathe. To wholly be.

The sky grows pale; a new day rises high.
The air stands still, the sands are scorched and dead.
I do not rush. The desert waits nearby.
The dunes drift on — like waves no river fed.
