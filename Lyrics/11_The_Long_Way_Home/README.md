# Альбом №11. «The Long Way Home»

The quiet road inward — a pass you cross only to find that the one you sought was yourself, a lighthouse that shines for no one, a love that outlasts worlds, and the small warmth of those who wait at home. Songs of the long way back — to yourself, and to the place you belong.
