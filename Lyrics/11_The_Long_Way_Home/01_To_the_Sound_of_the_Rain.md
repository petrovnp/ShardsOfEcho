# To the Sound of the Rain

**Album:** The Long Way Home  
**Track:** 01  
**Lyrics by:** Nickolay Petrov  
**Written:** 2026

---

Three days the fog has pressed against the pane,
the sky has blest the earth with pouring rain.
At night a cool wind sets the leaves to sway,
and breaks the murmur of the rain at play.

A leaden veil is drawn across the sky;
and clouds embrace the earth where far hills lie.
With heavy sighs the wind moves on, hard-pressed,
to flood my face with rain and strike my chest.

And with this wind I rise in flight,
to seek my answer out of sight,
toward summer's warmth and skies of blue,
toward sun and stars and moon's pale hue.

Line after line, the pen goes drifting slow,
through leaves of books forgotten long ago,
and on those pages but a shadow lay,
as in dim hollow sockets sleeps a day.

And I will leave with night, the two as one,
and pour myself as silver, and be gone,
and I will turn into a winged doe,
and higher, ever higher go,
and into boundless distance flow…
to the sound of the rain…
