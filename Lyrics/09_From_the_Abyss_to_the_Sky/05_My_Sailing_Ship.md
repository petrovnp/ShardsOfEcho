# My Sailing Ship

**Album:** From the Abyss to the Sky

**Lyrics by:** Nickolay Petrov  
**Written:** 2026

---

My sailing ship is lone on open ocean.
The way back home was lost long, long ago.
The water's mute, but in the brooding motion
I see a shadow trailing, soft and slow.

The far horizon darkened all around.
A gust struck hard, and made the canvas drone.
From deep below there rose a hollow sound,
and I stood frozen, stiff, and turned to stone.

The storm broke loose; the element ran wild.
A roaring squall came crashing like a wall.
The water boiled; the wreckage tossed and piled;
and darkness closed, a black and total pall.

The mast snapped clean in two with cracking strain.
The ninth great wave reared up, all crowned with spray.
I'm swept along the deadly surging main —
my hands clutched only air, and empty grey.

It seemed the end. Defeat. I'd lost it all.
But still my heart would not submit to night.
Let tempest roar — my spirit did not fall.
I stand. I'm here. And I am still alight!

And there, atop the tempest's raging crest,
I understood it all, and knew it plain:
this storm itself was that sea-call, that quest —
I AM this storm! In it, life's deep domain!

My sailing ship is lone on open ocean.
The storm has stilled; I am no more the same.
And morning light spreads wide in gentle motion;
I hold my course, and homeward now I aim.
