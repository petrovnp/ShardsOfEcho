# The Last Song

**Album:** Keepers of Time  
**Track:** 06  
**Lyrics by:** Nickolay Petrov  
**Written:** 2026

---

Once all the world resounded — I resounded.
My voice was once the beating of the earth.
I am the song that ages never bounded,
while seas and mountains lived and grew from birth.

I recall: the wind was once my very breathing;
I sang — and meadows bloomed in springtime air.
The rains ran down like tears, the hills enwreathing;
and all things played one music, everywhere.

I sound on still — but thinner now, a glimmer;
I melt away with every note I lend.
I once was storm — now my own voice grows dimmer;
and darkness in me deepens without end.

I cannot shape the final verse, the ending;
to sing it through — and all the world is gone.
I sink to silence now, the dark descending;
somewhere there dies my last and final dawn.

I brush the empty dark with one last breath.
My dying exhale — all I have to give.
No stars, no streams, no beauty — only death.
But still I sing. I cannot cease to live.
