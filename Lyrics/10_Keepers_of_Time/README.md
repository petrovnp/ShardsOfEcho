# Альбом №10. «Keepers of Time»

Old parables from the edge of creation — a Maker who departs, dragons set to keep the world and to keep time, and the price paid by those who hold the eternal watch. Of vast sleeps and fading songs, of a memory hammered into stars, and of the mercy to one that becomes a sentence upon all.
