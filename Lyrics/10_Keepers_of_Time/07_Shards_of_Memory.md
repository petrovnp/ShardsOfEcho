# Shards of Memory

**Track:** 07  
**Lyrics by:** Nickolay Petrov  
**Written:** 2026

The peaks are still. Night. Fog. A pale, full moon.
An old man walks the path he's always known.
The rock is dark; the road is rough, stone-strewn,
and only wind is moaning, low, alone.

He stirs the forge to life and feeds the flame.
He lifts no bar of iron — but soft light.
A warm, forgotten gift, without a name,
now trembles: someone's smile, voice, shape, and sight.

Burn on, bright star, you spark of vanished days,
first snow once cupped in palms, one winter's gleam.
In you, a fragment of my memory's blaze —
so whispered he, the smith, in moonlight's beam.

The ages passed. The veil of starlight glows —
like diamonds strewn through the dark, silent skies.
But he recalls no footstep, none he knows,
no words, no warm hands, and no lullabies.

Burn on, bright star, you spark of vanished days,
the scent of herbs along a forest road.
In you, a fragment of my memory's blaze —
so whispered he, the old man, by his abode.

But now, the final ember in his hand.
It beats, alive, and achingly his own.
He pressed it to his chest, could barely stand,
as something heavy rose, and turned to stone.

One stroke. The hammer fell. The hearth grew cold.
The anvil cooled; the silence, vast, lay sighing.
And one more star above the world took hold —
the brightest star of all, yet softly dying.

Burn on, bright star, you spark of vanished days...
in you, the shattered shards of memory's blaze...
